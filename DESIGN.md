# Fahmni Space-Dark Design System Specification (DESIGN.md)

This document is the visual and structural source of truth for the Fahmni Educational Platform (Center OS & portals). All UI updates, components, and layouts generated by AI coding agents must strictly adhere to the guidelines, colors, typography, and interactive rules described below.

---

## 🌌 1. Mood & Aesthetics
* **Theme:** Space-Dark Glassmorphism (Vibrant Neon Accents on Deep Cosmic Voids).
* **Atmosphere:** Immersive, clean, high-tech, trustworthy, and premium.
* **Layout Density:** Spaced and airy, using micro-padding and structured grids to avoid visual clutter.
* **Core Elements:** Rounded corners (`3xl` / `24px` for cards, `xl` / `12px` for buttons), thin semi-transparent borders, and blurred glass backdrops.

---

## 🎨 2. Color Palette (Semantic Tokens)

### 2.1 Surfaces & Backgrounds
* **Base Void Background:** `#060913` (Cosmic Black).
* **Primary Surface (Deep Glass):** `#0a0f1d` with `bg-white/[0.02]` overlay.
* **Elevated Card Glass:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.03)` with `backdrop-blur-md`.
* **Subtle Borders:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.06)` (thin, `1px`).
* **Glowing Inner Borders:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.12)`.

### 2.2 Accent Colors
* **Primary Brand Accent (Electric Cyan):** `#06b6d4` (glows with `cyan-500/20` shadow).
* **Secondary Brand Accent (Teal Glow):** `#14b8a6` (emerald-blue blend).
* **Success / Paid (Mint Emerald):** `#10b981` (tinted background: `bg-emerald-500/10`, text: `text-emerald-400`, border: `border-emerald-500/20`).
* **Warning / Expiring / Installment (Warm Amber):** `#f59e0b` (tinted background: `bg-amber-500/10`, text: `text-amber-400`, border: `border-amber-500/20`).
* **Danger / Expired / Debt (Neon Rose):** `#f43f5e` (tinted background: `bg-rose-500/10`, text: `text-rose-400`, border: `border-rose-500/20`).

### 2.3 Typography Colors
* **High Contrast (Primary Text):** `#f8fafc` (Slate-50).
* **Medium Contrast (Secondary/Description):** `#94a3b8` (Slate-400).
* **Muted Contrast (Labels/Toggles):** `#64748b` (Slate-500).

---

## ✍️ 3. Typography & Hierarchy
* **Font Family:** `Cairo`, Google Fonts (RTL Arabic first).
* **Scale & Hierarchy:**
  * **H1 (Main Section Headers):** `text-xl sm:text-2xl font-black text-white tracking-tight`
  * **H2 (Card Titles):** `text-sm sm:text-base font-bold text-gray-200`
  * **Body / Content:** `text-xs sm:text-sm font-medium text-gray-300`
  * **Labels / Small Badges:** `text-[10px] sm:text-xs font-black uppercase text-gray-500 tracking-wider`

---

## 🧩 4. Components & Layout Specifications

### 4.1 Cards & Containers
* Must use `bg-white/[0.02] border border-white/5 rounded-3xl p-6 backdrop-blur-md shadow-xl transition-all duration-300 hover:border-white/10 hover:shadow-2xl`.
* Avoid plain sharp corners or solid, flat background colors.

### 4.2 Interactive Elements & Buttons
* **Primary Glow Button:** `bg-gradient-to-r from-cyan-500 to-teal-500 hover:opacity-90 active:scale-[0.98] text-slate-950 font-black text-xs px-5 py-2.5 rounded-xl shadow-[0_0_15px_rgba(6,182,212,0.15)] transition-all cursor-pointer`.
* **Secondary Glass Button:** `bg-white/5 border border-white/10 hover:bg-white/10 text-white font-bold text-xs px-4 py-2 rounded-xl transition-all active:scale-[0.98] cursor-pointer`.
* **Destructive Accent Button:** `bg-rose-500/10 border border-rose-500/20 hover:bg-rose-500/20 text-rose-400 font-bold text-xs px-4 py-2 rounded-xl transition-all cursor-pointer`.

### 4.3 Badges (Status & Tags)
* Always use a padded box with low-opacity background color and matching high-contrast text.
* Example: `<span className="px-1.5 py-0.5 bg-emerald-500/10 text-emerald-400 border border-emerald-500/20 rounded text-[9px] font-bold">نشط ✅</span>`.

### 4.4 Modals & Slide-panels
* Must lock scroll and overlay with `bg-black/60 backdrop-blur-sm` backdrop.
* Main panel must have standard glassmorphic styling, smooth entry animation, and an absolute close button `X` on top.

---

## 🚫 5. Visual Guardrails
1. **NO Flat Primary Colors:** Never use raw red, bright blue, or lime green as solid fills. Always use curated HSL gradients or low-opacity tints.
2. **NO Hard Shadows:** Use wide, low-opacity shadows (`shadow-2xl` or `shadow-[0_0_20px_rgba(0,0,0,0.3)]`).
3. **DO NOT Wrap Layouts on Mobile:** For navigation and key tabs, prefer horizontal swipeable lists (`overflow-x-auto flex-nowrap`) over wrapping grid lists.
